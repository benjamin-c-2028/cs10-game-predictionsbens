# How to Open an Unverified App on macOS

Sometimes macOS blocks an app because Apple cannot verify the developer.

## Step 1: Try to open the app

Double-click the app.

You may see a message like:

> “Apple cannot check this app for malicious software”  
> or  
> “The developer cannot be verified.”

Click **Done** or **Cancel**.

## Step 2: Open Security settings

1. Click the **Apple menu** in the top-left corner.
2. Click **System Settings**.
3. Go to **Privacy & Security**.
4. Scroll down to the **Security** section.

## Step 3: Allow the app

You should see a message saying the app was blocked.

Click **Open Anyway**.

This option usually appears for about an hour after you first try to open the app.

## Step 4: Confirm

A warning will appear again.

Click **Open**.

You may need to enter your Mac password.

After this, the app is saved as an exception, so you should be able to open it normally next time.
